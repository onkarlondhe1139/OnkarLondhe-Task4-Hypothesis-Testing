## 👨‍💻 Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming analytical findings into a **compelling business story** and validating insights using **statistical testing techniques**.
The objective is to combine **data analysis, visualization, and hypothesis testing** to support **data-driven decision making**.

This task is part of the **Data Analytics Internship at ApexPlanet Software Pvt. Ltd.**

# 🎯 Objective

* Convert analytical results into a **clear business narrative**.
* Present insights using **data storytelling techniques**.
* Validate findings using **statistical hypothesis testing**.
* Deliver insights in a **business-friendly presentation format**.

# 📖 Data Storytelling

Data storytelling converts analytical results into a **structured business narrative**.

### Story Flow

1. Business problem
2. Data exploration
3. Key insights
4. Business recommendations

### Key Insights

* Pricing significantly influences **customer purchase behavior**.
* **Top-performing products drive most sales**.
* Sales vary across **different time periods**.
* Discounts can improve **product demand and sales performance**.

---

# 📊 Hypothesis Testing

Statistical tests were performed to validate analytical findings.

### Significance Level

α = **0.05**

---

## Test 1 – Price vs Sales Volume

**Hypothesis**

H₀: Price does not affect sales
H₁: Price affects sales

**Method Used**
Correlation Analysis

**Insight**
Price has a measurable relationship with sales behavior.

---

## Test 2 – Discount vs Sales

**Hypothesis**

H₀: Discounts do not increase sales
H₁: Discounts increase sales

**Method Used**
T-Test

**Insight**
Discount strategies positively influence product sales.

---

## Test 3 – Time vs Sales

**Hypothesis**

H₀: Sales are equal across time periods
H₁: Sales vary across time periods

**Method Used**
ANOVA

**Insight**
Sales patterns change depending on the time period.
